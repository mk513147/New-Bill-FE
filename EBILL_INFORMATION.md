# EBill - Complete Business Management Platform

## Overview
EBill is a comprehensive all-in-one platform designed for small and medium businesses to manage their complete operations - from billing and inventory to customer management and financial analytics.

---

## Core Purpose & Mission
EBill helps growing businesses automate their operations, track finances accurately, and make data-driven decisions. It consolidates multiple tools into one unified platform, eliminating the need for juggling spreadsheets and disparate software.
